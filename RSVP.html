Notes

To ensure there are no interruptions, the Venue gates will be locked during the ceremony. If you arrive after the ceremony has started, you can park your vehicle on the road outside the venue and walk in.

After the Ceremony has finished and the gates are re-opened, we ask that you move your vehicle to the parking lot inside the venue.

We will be providing a frozen drink machine as well as a few limited beer options. Guests may bring their own alcohol, however it must be given to the bartender where it becomes communal property.

COVID
To ensure the safety of everyone in attendance

  • we ask that guests wear a face mask indoors at all times unless seated and eating
  • Hand sanitizer will be available throughout the venue
  • Guests are asked to social distance whenever possible, and are encouraged to sit at the outdoor seating
