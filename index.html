We Love Each Other

Our Story

  • Love At First Swipe

    November 11, 2018

    This is the day we met online. Even over text, there was an instant connection.

  • First Date

    November 14, 2018

    After a few days of talking we decided to go on our first date. Ryan cooked Ashley a dinner of chicken, green beans, and rice. Ryan asked Ashley to check if her chicken was fully cooked, as he didn't want to give her salmonella. We laughed about it and now it is one of our many inside jokes.

  • Made It Official

    November 15, 2018

    The next day, we went to a reggae concert together. We had a blast, and at the end of the night we officially began our journey together.

  • Bought a House

    April 13, 2019

    Sick of apartment life, we searched the DFW metroplex for a place to call our own. In april 2019, we found a house with awesome history and an amazing view. We decided to go for it!

  • Proposal

    January 1, 2020

    We were at Lake Tahoe in Nevada on a much-needed vacation, surrounded by beautiful scenery, when Ryan popped the question. At first, Ashley was so surprised she didn't understand what Ryan was doing! It was the best beginning to a new year, and the rest of our lives.

  • Wedding!

    November 14, 2020

    Two years later, we're getting married!
